Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI determine the best diet plan for me?

The AI analyzes data such as age, weight, height, gender, dietary preferences, health goals, and any specific health conditions (like diabetes or allergies). Using this data, it recommends a customized meal plan designed to meet your nutritional needs and personal goals.

Can the AI accommodate specific dietary restrictions or preferences?

Yes! The AI can tailor diet plans based on your dietary preferences, such as vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or low-carb diets. It can also adjust to any food allergies, intolerances, or personal restrictions you specify.

Can the AI adapt my diet recommendations as my goals change?

Absolutely! As you make progress or if your goals change (e.g., weight loss to muscle gain), you can update your profile. The AI will adjust your diet plan accordingly to ensure it aligns with your new objectives.

How often does the AI update my meal recommendations?

The AI can provide daily meal recommendations and adjust them based on your activity level, current weight, and other health data you enter. You can also choose weekly or monthly updates depending on your preferences and the nature of your health goals.
